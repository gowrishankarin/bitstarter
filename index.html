Social change can happen only through education and the world is striving for that change. Mothers from the poor slums of Bombay shares the same goal of providing quality education to their children with the mothers of the richest suburbs of Copenhagen. Modern day educational system achieved miracles in a collective setup but did not address the upliftment of men individually. Technological advancements like MOOCs solves this problem greatly, rapidly and effectively. In this revolution one should not forget the humanity's good old habit of reading books and its merry.

The books that the world calls immoral are books that show the world its own shame.

Oscar Wilde

Today, people are happy sharing their photos, delighted by sheer number of retweets, excited when someone pin their creation. Social media made all those things trivially possible but very few want to talk about books they have read(If at all..). Even If they are willing to talk, it is among their confined circle of friends and relatives, thoughts are conveyed but not recorded, never shared, eventually forgotten. How many of us are aware of the books written by Charlotte Bronte, how many of us even know about the Franz Kafka and his burnt books, how many of us heard about Thiruvalluvar and his 3 ways of living life from his Thrikkural. These men did not write those books for obtaining literature degree in elite educational institutes but for everyone to read.

FAQ

What is Bibliotheqam?
Bibliotheqam is Not For Profit initiative to encourage reading habit among its users. It is a platform where people can see statistics like user acceptance, quality of experience and impact on society/individual.
Who can join Bibliotheqam?
Anyone who has interest in reading books and willing to share information about the book he/she had read.
What is special about Bibliotheqam?
Its Users.
What is Bibliotheqam's goals are?
Bibliotheqam should be identified as the platform which brings people and books together. Today, individuals are identified and judged by their Photographs(Facebook), Profession(LinkedIn), Blogs(Twitter, Word Press) which are important but Bibliotheqam's vision is to portray an individual by what he had read and his transformation due to his reading.
I am a voracious reader and I am already motivated enough to read, Will it be beneficial If I join?
You are gifted, You are THE ONE who is going to change many through Bibliotheqam.
How Bibliotheqam verifies an individual really read a given book which he claims he had read?
Bibliotheqam's biggest challenges is verification of one's knowledge and intellect acquired from a particular book. Although, there are ways to address this issue by short interactive quizzes, Bibliotheqam wish to trust its members' inputs.
Can I buy books from Bibliotheqam?
No, Bibliotheqam's interests are not to indulge in any sort of commercial activity, It's primary goal is to build a community of book lovers.
How Bibliotheqam will raise funds for sustenance?
This is a social cause, society will take care of operational expenses through donations. If required, there are many options to explore... by then the platform will add significant value to the people to have it alive.

Who is behind Bibliotheqam?
I am a book lover, who cannot find enough time to read books in this fast paced lifestyle. I believe proper motivation and recognition will change direction and lead to prioritize reading.
